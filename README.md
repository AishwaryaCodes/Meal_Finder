# ğŸ› ï¸ **How It Works**

1. **Search for Meals:** Enter a search term in the search bar and hit submit.
2. **View Results:** The app will display a list of meals that match your query, complete with images and meal names.
3. **Explore Recipes:** Click on a meal to view its ingredients and instructions.
4. **Get Cooking:** Follow the recipe steps to prepare your delicious meal!
